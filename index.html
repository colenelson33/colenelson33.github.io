Class-Rate

Similar to RateMyProfessor, Class-Rate was made for high school students, for high school students. An intuitive UI makes the app pleasant to scroll through, and detailed descriptions of each class allow users to fully understand what they may be getting themselves into.

Handi-Counter

Not wanting to formally start the decline in my passion for golf, I decided to create HandiCount. This Apple Watch only app allowed me to privatly keep track of my golf handicap and get good read on how good at golf I actually am. It is available for free on the App Store, and its simple design allows the user to keep track of their score and submit (only if they want to) their score to the app.

Incoming!

Inspired by retro asteriod games, Incoming! is an exciting (unfinished) game anyone can play. Keep an eye out for special abilities and nukes popping up on your screen.
